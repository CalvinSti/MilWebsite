What is Audio Media?

Audio media uses sound to convey information. It includes dialogue, music, sound effects, and even silence to set pacing and emphasize points.

Here's an example of Stereo-Imaging in the form of a very handsome man whispering in your ear

What is Motion Media?

Motion media uses visuals to simulate movement. It can represent the flow of information and evoke emotions through speed, direction, and timing.

Design Principles

Audio Media

  • Mixing: Balancing multiple sound elements.
  • Transitions: Smoothly shifting between segments.
  • Stereo Imaging: Adding depth with left and right channels.
